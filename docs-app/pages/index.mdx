# SecureLens Documentation

Welcome to **SecureLens** — the enterprise Data & AI Trust Platform that helps you discover, classify, and govern sensitive data across your organization.

## What is SecureLens?

SecureLens is a cloud-based platform that acts as an **active governance gateway** between your data sources and AI systems. Simply connect your data sources, and SecureLens automatically:

- **Discovers sensitive data** — Find PII, PHI, PCI, and 60+ data types across all your systems
- **Classifies with AI** — Intelligent classification that understands context, not just patterns
- **Enforces policies** — Define rules that are enforced before data reaches AI systems
- **Audits everything** — Complete trail of who accessed what, when, and why

## Why SecureLens?

As enterprises adopt AI and LLMs, they face critical challenges:

| Challenge | How SecureLens Helps |
|-----------|---------------------|
| PII leaking to AI | Automatic detection and redaction before data reaches LLMs |
| Compliance violations | Policy enforcement at the gateway level |
| No visibility into AI usage | Complete audit trail of all AI interactions |
| Data quality issues | Automated quality scoring and ROT detection |
| Manual compliance work | Automated reports for GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, and more |

## Key Capabilities

### Data Discovery & Classification
Connect your databases, cloud storage, and SaaS applications. SecureLens automatically scans and classifies sensitive data using AI-powered detection.

### Governance Policies
Define access controls, redaction rules, and data handling policies. Policies are enforced in real-time as data flows through the platform.

### AI Gate
Route your LLM requests through SecureLens. Monitor prompts, redact sensitive data automatically, and maintain a complete audit trail.

### Compliance Automation
Built-in support for GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, SOX, PCI-DSS, and custom frameworks. Generate compliance reports with one click.
